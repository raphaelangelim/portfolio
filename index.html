Case Study

Injected Ad Component

Performance Optimization

Problem: Injecting an ad unit into an existing article page without harming UX, performance, or scroll behavior.

Injected Ad Component
Fixed height reserved to avoid CLS (Layout Shift)

Approach: The component is injected at a logical content break with reserved height to ensure layout stability during load.

Results: Stable layout, predictable tracking, and improved viewability without impacting LCP.

Analysis & Strategy
